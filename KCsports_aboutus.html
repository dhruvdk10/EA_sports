ABOUT US

KC Football Academy was established in 2025 and within a short span of time, it has earned a top spot among the sports academies in Gurugram and is ranked in the top 10 nationwide.

Our academy is located in the heart of Gurugram, Haryana, India, and is dedicated to nurturing young football talent and promoting the sport at all levels. We provide a platform for aspiring footballers to develop their skills, participate in competitive matches, and receive professional coaching.

At KC Football Academy, we are passionate about football and committed to creating a positive and inclusive environment for all players. Our experienced coaches are dedicated to helping each player reach their full potential, both on and off the field.

Our team of experienced coaches is committed to nurturing talent and helping players reach their full potential. We offer a range of programs, including youth training, competitive teams, and specialized coaching sessions.

We believe in the importance of teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship, and we strive to instill these values in all our players. Our academy provides a supportive and encouraging environment where players can learn, grow, and develop their skills.

We are proud to have a diverse community of players, coaches, and supporters who share our passion for football. Our academy is not just about training; it's about building friendships, fostering teamwork, and creating lasting memories.

Join us at KC Football Academy and be part of a community that shares your passion for football. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, we have something for everyone. Let's play, have fun, and enjoy the game together!

OUR MISSION

To be a leading football academy that inspires and develops the next generation of football stars.
